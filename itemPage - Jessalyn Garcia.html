Switching Roles

By Jessalyn Garcia

This project is an augmented reality (AR) experience that aims to personify an existing film building. The idea behind the project is to bring the building to life, similar to how the people inside the building bring stories to life. By using AR technology, viewers can experience an interactive and immersive view of the film building, where they can see the building come alive and interact with it in unique ways. Overall, Switching Roles aims to showcase the potential of AR in creating engaging and dynamic experiences that go beyond traditional forms of storytelling.
