Famous People

Burbank is known as the Media City as there are a lot of popular people in Burbank. The topic of the Tour is Popular people, and the main purpose of the tour is to show the places where these popular people are associated with. Popular people are Architects, Actors, Actresses, Martial artists, Pioneers, Racers, Journalists and so on. They all have connections with spaces/locations from Burbank. Burbank has childhood homes, car garage, restaurants, historic museums and so on. Since it is known as the media capacity, there are people in the history of Burbank who are honored, and they are the popular people in the Burbank history. They succeed in their lives, popular and they are from Burbank. Having different types of careers in the Burbank’s history, the honorable people have various accomplishments. The spaces that they used in the past are the ones that people are using these days.

AR experience shows the relationship between the places in Burbank with the popular people with their career, why they are famous and what type of relationship they have with these specific places. AR experiences mainly shows what connection that the popular people have with the locations from Burbank , their careers and their hobbies that are related to Burbank areas. The triggers for the AR are the house numbers where they had relationships with. Scanning the Triggers allows the users to experience in seeing the popular people along with their careers, their creations and their hobbies.
