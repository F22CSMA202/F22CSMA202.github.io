About The Project

"Mapping Burbank" is a unique digital platform that provides an immersive tour of the Burbank community. Our mission is to bring to light the hidden stories and overlooked monuments of this city, which has long been overshadowed by its reputation as a hub of the film and television industry.

History

This section showcases Burbank's rich history, which includes major transportation and military projects, as well as a deep Native American and Spanish Heritage.

Culture

This section showcases Burbank's rich culture, which includes its sprawling entertainment industry alongside a Southern Californian car culture and unique architectural backdrop.

Ecology

This section showcases Burbank's rich ecology, which includes native flora and fauna endemic to Southern California as well as the region's wine production.
