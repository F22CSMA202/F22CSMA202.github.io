Houses

Burbank, California, is a vibrant city full of unique houses with diverse architectural styles often overlooked by many residents and visitors. These houses are integral to the region's history and culture, as well as its character and identity. They provide a memorable backdrop to the city and are essential to what makes Burbank special. ARtifacts is an exciting journey into the diverse and vibrant houses and architectural styles that characterize the wonderful city of Burbank. With a fun, informative, and interactive approach, visitors will be able to dive into Burbank's unique and captivating history of architecture.
Experience ARtifacts for yourself! Discover the classic and modern architectural styles of 12 remarkable homes as you walk or drive on this interactive trail. Feel the history of the past and present as you appreciate the beauty of the buildings, four of which are historical residential landmarks. Unlock a unique viewing experience of a common household item from the period of the house by scanning a QR code at these four stops, and immerse yourself in this period by exploring these items' intricate details as if you were right there in the house! These virtual ARtifacts will feature animations, sound effects, and other engaging elements to make your experience of Burbank even more exciting.
