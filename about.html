About the Project

"Mapping Burbank" is a unique digital platform that provides an immersive tour of the Burbank community. Our mission is to bring to light the hidden stories and overlooked monuments of this city, which has long been overshadowed by its reputation as a hub of the film and television industry.

Our team of Woodbury Students have worked together to create a self-guided, story-based tour that showcases the rich history and cultural heritage of Burbank. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with traditional storytelling methods to create an engaging and informative experience for visitors.

With "Mapping Burbank," you'll have the opportunity to explore this vibrant community at your own pace, learning about its fascinating history and discovering hidden gems along the way. Whether you're a local resident or just visiting, our platform is the perfect way to experience the true essence of Burbank.

We're dedicated to presenting the community of Burbank in a way that is both informative and enjoyable. Join us on this journey and discover the untold stories of this remarkable city.
