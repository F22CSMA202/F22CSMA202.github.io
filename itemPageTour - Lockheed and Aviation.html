Lockheed & Aviation

Historical aviation in Burbank begins with the name of Allan Haines Lockheed American engineer, aviator, and businessman Allan Haines Loughead created the Alco Hydro-Aeroplane Company and his brother, Malcolm Loughead, which became Lockheed Corporation. Loughead legally changed their last name to Lockheed to avoid confusion and gave their aircraft foundered corporation their previous last name.
