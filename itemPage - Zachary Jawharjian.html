Downtown Burbank AR

By Zachary Jawharjian

This AR project, entitled Downtown Burbank, uses vintage postcards of Burbankâ€™s San Fernando Blvd projected onto abstract easel forms that are representative of the city scape. Each easel represents an existing building along the street, and the postcards are scattered to create multiple views in the space. The atmosphere is further enhanced with street ambient noise to make the user feel as if they were walking through the city while on campus.
