Television Industry

Burbank is the hot spot for filming studios. Studios like Warner Bros, Walt Disney Studios, and NBC have been in Burbank since the 1950s and 60s. Burbank also has many key locations that are used in popular shows that we all know and love. The filming studios have been the base point for shows like I Love Lucy. The first stop of the tour starts at WB Studios where you are able to be in the same location I Love Lucy was filmed. The second location will be in the mall on the second level and a clip of Gilmore Girls must be matched by using pointers like the escalator. The third location is a clip from The Office where Michael gets lost after being left at the gas station bathroom by Jim. Michael tries to eat at a hotdog restaurant and gets denied. One must place him in front of Larry’s Chili Dog.
