The Chronicle

By Hay Thi Ko

The monument shows that Burbank has famous people along with the places that they had relationships with. Burbank has historic places where these popular people used, and the monument is to honor them with the spaces that they had relationships with by collecting the pieces that are related to these spaces. The purpose of the monument is to create a historic community with famous people and their spaces and giving information about how these monument pieces are related to the famous people along with their spaces. The foundation of the monument is Burbank city with the streets in Burbank since all the historic buildings and people were in Burbank. The idea of the monument is that the symbolizations of the buildings that popular people associated with are in the exact direction and location in Burbank City, creating the historic community, Landscape monument with the outline of the Burbank city.
